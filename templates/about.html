Contextual Camouflage

A [web] app that uses mapping technology to gather mental health data anonymously based on user input and present it in real time via a heat map.
The [web] app will also provide resources and present current research. Users can opt-in to have their (non-)personal data used in research. There will also be a public installation of the heat map projected at target location(s).
